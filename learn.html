1A, 1B, 1C

  1. Do not go to bed with wet hair.
  2. Blow dry on cool settings.
  3. Do a weekly hair mask.
  4. Avoid products that contain sulfates.
  5. Use a microfiber towel.

2A, 2B, 2C

  1. Use a frizz-fighting shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Deep condition.
  3. Air dry to minimize frizz-causing friction.
  4. Don't brush.
  5. Scrunch hair to style.
  6. Try sea salt.
  7. Infuse your waves with oil.

3A, 3B, 3C:

  1. Pre-shampoo before actually washing. 3B curls need extra moisture to protect against dryness and frizz.
  2. Shampoo just once or twice a week.
  3. Squish to condish.
  4. Deep condition frequently.
  5. Don't brush. Use a comb.
  6. Experiment with the LOC or LCO methods.
  7. Stay away from using heat to style.
  8. Sleep in a pineapple.

4A, 4B, 4C:

  1. Moisturizing your hair will be the most important thing! 4c hair, like all curly hair types, is naturally dry.
  2. Be gentle. 4c hair is the most fragile hair type.
  3. Take advantage of protective styling.
  4. Don't neglect scalp care!
  5. Give your ends a little TLC.
  6. Trim regularly.
